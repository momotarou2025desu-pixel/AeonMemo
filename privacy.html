Privacy Policy (Aeon Memo)

Last updated: 2025-12-23

Notes

Aeon Memo stores the notes you type locally on your device. Aeon Memo does not upload the content of your notes to any external server.

Advertising

Aeon Memo displays banner ads provided by Google.

Google may collect and use information such as device identifiers (including Advertising ID), IP address, approximate location, and ad interaction data in order to provide, personalize, and measure advertisements.

Data collected by Google is handled in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Analytics

Aeon Memo does not use analytics or tracking other than what may be required by the advertising provider for ad delivery and measurement.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your notes remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app. Uninstalling the app removes the app’s local data.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us via the GitHub repository Issues page.
